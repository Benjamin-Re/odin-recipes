Pancake recipie

Pancakes can also be salty.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Apple
  • Sugar
  • Milk(can be lactose free)
  1. Mix Flour (~200g), Sugar (one table spoon) and Milk (~100 ml).
  2. Heat oil in pan.
  3. Cut apple.
  4. Put dough in pan so it covers the whole pan but is still very thinly spread.
  5. Flip as soon as you can.
  6. Put apple pieces on the pancake. (You can add anything really).
